History of Gaza

In recent decades, Gaza has been deeply affected by repeated wars and military operations involving Israel. Ongoing conflict, occupation, and blockade have led to widespread destruction of homes, infrastructure, and essential services. These wars have created a prolonged humanitarian crisis that continues to impact everyday life in Gaza.
